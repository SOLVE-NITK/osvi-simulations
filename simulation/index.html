Heat Treatment Of Steels

Aim: To give various heat treatments like annealing, normalizing and hardening to a 0.4%C steel and study the effect of these treatments on structure and properties.

Apparatus Required:

Steel Samples, polish papers, belt grinder, furnace, Rockwell hardness tester, microscope

Procedure:

2024 SOLVE - The Virtual Lab @ NITK Surathkal, Department of Water Resources & Ocean Engineering.
