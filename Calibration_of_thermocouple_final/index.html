CALIBRATION OF THERMOCOUPLE

Objective

To calibrate a thermocouple for a particular metal

Appratus used

Crucible, Furnace, Thermocouple, EMF Indicator, Clock, Observational Reference Table

Step 1

Pure Metal pieces are melted in a crucible inside one of the crucible furnaces

Step 2

Join the thermocouple and emf indicator

Step 3

Dip thermocouple connected with wire into melted metal

Step 4

Start noting down the emf readings

Step 5

Analyse the readings and make the graph

Step 6

Plot the graph and Repeat the experiment

Step 7

Calculation of Error of Thermocouple for Zn metal
