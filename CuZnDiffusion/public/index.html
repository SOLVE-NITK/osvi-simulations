Diffusion of copper in zinc

Objective

To observe the microstructure of diffused zinc into copper

Appratus used

Bell grinder, Crucible furnance, FeCl2, Velvet polish paper

Step 1

Pure Zinc pieces are melted in a crucible inside one of the crucible furnaces

Step 2

Using sandpaper the copper rod is cleaned to remove the oxide layer

Step 3

Dip the rod in the molten zinc

Step 4

Remove the solidified material and cut a vertical section centrally parallel to the axis of the copper rod.

Step 5

Bell grinder polish

Step 6

Velvet polish paper

Step 7

Rinse the metal with Fecl2

Step 8

Observations
